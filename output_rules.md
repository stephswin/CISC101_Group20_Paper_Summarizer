# Expert Summary
- Technical explanation with discipline-specific terminology.
- Includes methodological rigor and statistical details.

# Lay Summary
- Simplified explanation for non-experts.
- Focuses on real-world implications and plain language.

# Mini-Glossary
- **p-value**: A measure of statistical significance.
- **Regression model**: A mathematical method to predict outcomes.
- **Context window**: The maximum text length the system can process at once.
